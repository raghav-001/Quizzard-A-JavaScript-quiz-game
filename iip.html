About Quizzard



Technology is having a vast development over the years and this project aims to make good use of it for the young population of the nation. The main aim of this project is to create an interactive quiz based website for students to gain knowledge with fun through technology. Many schools and colleges prefer conducting quizzes to test the knowledge of students, hence highlighting the fact that it is a good input for knowledge. A few topics that are planned are general knowledge, physics, and first aid. Apart from the fact that education is very important, it is important to be updated about the general stuff happening outside the world of education as well.
